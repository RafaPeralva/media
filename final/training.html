Training:

Training for a fight requires a lot of effort both physically and mentally from fighters. It may take months to prepare for a single fight, not counting the years that are spent training before you can get matched up for a fight. However, many fighters will tell you that training is not the worst part, dieting and cutting weight are. Fighters drop crazy amount of weight for a fight, I've seen some dropping 15-20lbs in about a month. That requires a lot of discipline and will power from them.
