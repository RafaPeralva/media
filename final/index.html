A fight gallery

I have been doing martial arts in some way/shape/form since first grade. I started by training Judo in my school in Brazil and did so until 6th grade. In 2017 I started training Muay Thai. Last year I was able to start helping out with my gym and have been able to help with training and fights. I thought it would be a good idea to portray the fighters in a gallery that showed more than their "angry" faces but that also showed how hard they train and how much heart they put into this lifestyle.
